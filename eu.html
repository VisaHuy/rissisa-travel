What didn't we see there?

According to VTsIOM polls, 95% of Russians dream of going somewhere, but only 36% planning to spend their holidays in their home country. Like, what have we not seen here at home? In fact, Russia - this is a whole universe with the gentle sea of the south, the dense forests of the Sayans and the harsh ice of the Putorana Plateau. AND you can still see all these beauties without millions in your account, a passport and many hours of flights. Like, for example, Vera Bashmakova, a brave young mother, who took three children in her arms, sat them down in her "Lada" and drove 20 thousand kilometers in her native country. We have selected and described some interesting places worthy of your vacation.

Curonian spit

URL

park-kosa.ru

Here, in the middle of forests and sand dunes, you can see two water horizon - the calm Curonian Lagoon on one side and the rippling waves of the Baltic seas on the other. A unique natural area on the edge of the Russian enclave.

The Kaliningrad region does not end there. For traveler and explorer in the same neighborhood - the westernmost point of Russia, The Baltic Spit - and the German heritage of a placer of small seaside towns. Atmosphere of these places eliminates the fuss, dipping into the tranquility of nature and the smell of steel, cool seas.

Kola

URL

National Geographic

Almost the entire peninsula is located beyond the Arctic Circle. Saami tundra, from which south taiga, and to the north - the Arctic Ocean, pretending to be the Barents Sea.

Perhaps you watched Zvyagintsev and even heard the history of the Arctic festival in Teriberka. Perhaps the word "Khibiny" did not remain under the snow of school memories about geography lessons. Perhaps you were not interested in the ultra-deep well, and from apatites you have long been covered with apathy. But your dream is to see the northern lights starts to come true with a ticket to Murmansk.

Altai

URL

Facebook

Altai is one of the most beautiful places in Russia. First of all, because of the mountains: if you drive along the ridge, you will see slopes strewn with pine trees, mountain rivers and lakes. And if you open the windows in the car, you can get acquainted with the invisible by the miracle of these places - mountain air.

The climate in Altai is temperate, so it is best to go here in summer. So you will see all the variety of local flora and fauna. Moose roam the forests of Altai, over the ridges eagles fly, and roe deer graze on the plains. And the famous manuls are also inhabitants of the Altai the edges.

Winter Baikal

URL

https://vk.com/baikalmile

Everyone knows Baikal as the largest lake in the world. Many also know that this is the largest source of fresh water and one of the most beautiful places in Russia.

Of course, it's all true. But Baikal is still an ideal place for competitions skiing. This is a sport where the skier ties himself to the motorcycle, and the tandem tries to develop as much speed as possible on the ice. March 2019 year at the festival "Baikal Mile" a world record was set - 197.011 km / h.

Karelia

URL

http://vodlozero.ru/

Siberia ends not in the Urals, but in Karelia: forming the taiga Siberian larch does not grow west of Vodlozero. But here she swings 30 meters - the forests of the Karelian national parks, because of the impenetrable swamps, have never known the axe. Some pines are more than half a millennium old. Touch a living being who has seen the sun before Ivan the Terrible saw it. In the virgin forest for a hundred kilometers you will not find a path. And on rare paths trees a couple of meters from the ground are marked with bear claws. So that everyone knows who is the boss here.
